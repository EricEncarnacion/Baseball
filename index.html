A Brief History of Baseball

Baseball grew out of a game called “town ball” (sometimes just called base) which was a very informal game that had grown out of the British game rounders. The rules of Baseball were originally formalized by the New York Knickerbockers Baseball Club starting in 1845 who played in the first recorded baseball game in 1846.
